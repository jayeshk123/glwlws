About Green Leaf Wild Life

We as the team of Greenleaf are nature loving enthusiasts with similar liking and that is trailingthrough the varied beauty of nature. We belong to the different backgrounds but for more than 10 years we are following nature through camping, photography, nature presentations etc. and one fine day in 2012 we decided to develope our common interest into serious hobby. We presently organize Wild Life tours with Tiger Safari as the main attraction, Bird Watching tours, Nature as Stress buster tour for Corporates & Family categories.
